# RISE Trader - Business Requirements

## Functional Requirements
The system must satisfy the following functional requirements for Version 1:

### 1. Data Collection
* **Market Universe**: The system must monitor and collect intraday market data only for the active NIFTY 50 stock universe.
* **Intraday Data**: The system must fetch intraday price and volume data at regular intervals during market hours.
* **NIFTY Reference Index**: The system must collect index data for the NIFTY 50 to compute relative metrics.

### 2. Indicator Calculation
* **Volume Ratio**: Calculate the current day's cumulative trading volume relative to its historical average volume (e.g., average of the past 10–20 days) at the same time of day.
* **VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)**: Calculate the intraday VWAP for each stock and determine if the current price is trading above or below it.
* **ORB (Opening Range Breakout)**: Identify the high and low prices established during the opening range (e.g., first 15 or 30 minutes of the trading session) and detect breakout occurrences when the price crosses these boundaries.
* **Relative Strength (RS)**: Compare the intraday performance of each stock relative to the NIFTY 50 index to identify outperforming assets.

### 3. Scoring and Ranking
* **Scoring Rules**: Apply objective scoring rules to rank stocks based on the calculated quantitative indicators.
* **Recommendation Selection**: Filter and select the top 2 ranked stocks as the recommendations of the day.
* **Confidence Scoring**: Assign a confidence score (e.g., scale of 1-10 or a percentage) based on the number and strength of quantitative rules triggered.
* **Reasoning Generation**: For each of the recommended stocks, generate a text explanation outlining which indicator criteria and conditions were met to trigger the recommendation.

### 4. Notifications
* **Telegram Delivery**: Format and transmit the daily top 2 stock recommendations, confidence scores, and selection explanations via Telegram.
* **Execution Timing**: The notification must be triggered and delivered dynamically when the breakout or ranking criteria are satisfied during trading hours.

### 5. Logging and Performance Tracking
* **Persistent Log**: Record every recommendation generated by the system, including:
  * Generation timestamp
  * Stock symbol
  * Indicator values at the time of recommendation
  * Confidence score
  * Selection explanation
* **Paper Trading Database/File**: Store the paper trading outcomes (theoretical entry price, theoretical exit price, and performance metrics) to evaluate strategy efficacy.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

### 1. Reliability and Availability
* **Market Hours Uptime**: The system must operate reliably without interruption during active Indian stock market hours (09:15 AM to 03:30 PM IST, Monday through Friday).
* **Graceful Degradation**: In the event of data feed interruptions, the system must fail gracefully, log the error, and notify the user without crashing.

### 2. Accuracy and Performance
* **Processing Latency**: The scoring, ranking, and recommendation pipeline must process all NIFTY 50 stocks in near-real-time to ensure recommendations remain actionable.
* **Indicator Precision**: Calculations for VWAP, Volume Ratio, and RS must match standard quantitative definitions.

### 3. Maintainability
* **Separation of Concerns**: The business logic for data collection, indicator calculation, and ranking must be decoupled to allow future rule updates.
* **Configuration Management**: System parameters (such as the Telegram API token, NIFTY 50 ticker list, and indicator thresholds) must be configurable without modifying the core logic.

### 4. Security & Compliance
* **Access Credentials**: Telegram API keys and data provider tokens must be kept secure.
* **Compliance**: The system is strictly a personal learning project and decision-support tool. It must not violate any exchange or developer terms of service.

## Project Scope

### In-Scope (Version 1)
* **Target Universe**: NIFTY 50 stocks only.
* **Key Indicators**: Volume Ratio, VWAP, Opening Range Breakout (ORB), and Relative Strength.
* **Primary Output**: Top 2 stock recommendations per run, confidence score, and qualitative explanation.
* **Communication Channel**: Telegram alerts.
* **Validation Method**: Strict paper-trading mode (dry-run/logging).

### Out-of-Scope (Version 1)
* AI/ML-generated predictions or neural network modeling.
* News sentiment analysis or social media scraping.
* Broker API integrations (no automated order routing).
* Automatic trading or live order execution.
* Real-money trading or capital deployment.
* Portfolio management (e.g., position sizing, portfolio rebalancing).
* Live web dashboard or graphical user interface (other than Telegram alerts and database/text logs).

---

## Assumptions
* Intraday and historical data for the NIFTY 50 index and its constituent stocks are available via free or low-cost APIs (such as yfinance or similar).
* The user has access to a reliable internet connection and an environment capable of running scheduled tasks.
* Telegram services remain active and available to receive programmatic message broadcasts.

---

## Constraints
* **Operational Constraint**: The system is legally and operationally restricted to paper trading validation. It cannot place live orders or trade real capital.
* **Hardware/Runtime Constraint**: The system will run as a lightweight automation tool and should not require high-performance computing clusters or expensive infrastructure.
* **Universe Constraint**: The scanning mechanism is hard-constrained to the NIFTY 50 index constituents; expansion to broader markets is not allowed in Version 1.
